## üìö NBA Metrics Reference

### Player Efficiency Rating (PER)
- **Formula**: (Points + Rebounds + Assists + Steals + Blocks - Missed FG - Missed FT - Turnovers) / Minutes √ó 100
- **League Average**: 15.0
- **Interpretation**:
  - < 10: Poor
  - 10-15: Below average
  - 15-20: Above average
  - 20-25: All-Star
  - > 25: MVP candidate

### True Shooting Percentage (TS%)
- **Formula**: Points / (2 √ó (FGA + 0.44 √ó FTA))
- **Interpretation**:
  - < 50%: Below average
  - 50-55%: Average
  - 55-60%: Good
  - > 60%: Elite

### Offensive/Defensive Rating
- **Formula**: (Points / Possessions) √ó 100
- **Interpretation**:
  - ORtg > 110: Good offense
  - DRtg < 105: Elite defense
  - Net Rating (ORtg - DRtg) > 5: Championship contender
